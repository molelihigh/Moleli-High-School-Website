About Moleli High School

Moleli High School is a Zimbabwean secondary school located near Norton in Mashonaland West Province, approximately 80 kilometers southwest of Harare.

The school offers both Ordinary Level and Advanced Level education and provides boarding facilities for students.

In 2018, the school was ranked 11th in Zimbabwe's Top 100 O' Level Schools with a pass rate of 92.3%.

History

Moleli High School was established in 1962 by Ronald E. Sellers of the Methodist Church in Zimbabwe.

The school was named after the late Methodist Reverend Modumedi Moleli.

It is a Methodist mission school and a sister school to Sandringham High School.

The school motto is:

"Tsvakai Chokwadi Kuyamura Vamwe"

This means: "Seek the truth to help others."
