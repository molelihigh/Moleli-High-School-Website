Our Story

Moleli High School is one of Zimbabwe’s respected Methodist mission schools, located near Norton in Mashonaland West Province.

Established in 1962 by Ronald E. Sellers, the school has continuously produced disciplined, intelligent and visionary students.

Moleli High School offers both O-Level and A-Level education, boarding facilities and a strong academic environment.
