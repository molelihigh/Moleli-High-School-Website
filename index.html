About Moleli

Excellence and Discipline Since 1962

Shaping Future Leaders

Moleli High School is one of Zimbabwe’s respected mission schools, located near Norton in Mashonaland West Province.

The school provides quality education, strong Christian values, leadership development and a disciplined learning environment.

Students excel academically, spiritually and socially, preparing them for a successful future.
